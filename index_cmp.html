Welcome to Cornell Music Production !

Cornell Music Production is a club at Cornell University dedicated to exploring the world of music production and technology. Our members come from a variety of backgrounds and interests, and we strive to create a supportive and inclusive community for anyone who loves music and technology.

About Our Club

We aspire to make people who have even a minor interest in the world of production grow their confidence in what they can create on thier own.
