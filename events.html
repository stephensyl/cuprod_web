Our Socials

Club Cornell Music Production regularly hosts social events that allow members to connect, collaborate, and share their passion for music. These socials provide a relaxed and informal setting where members can network, exchange ideas, and find potential collaborators for their projects. The events often feature live performances,listening sessions, team mentor games and beat battles.

Moments in the Studio

One of the club's standout initiatives is "Moments in the Studio," a series of sessions where members can work together in the club's dedicated music production studio. These sessions offer hands-on experience with professional-grade equipment and software, guided by more experienced members or guest producers. "Moments in the Studio" is designed to be an inclusive and educational environment where everyone, from beginners to advanced producers, can learn and grow. Members have the opportunity to experiment with different genres, techniques, and workflows, all while receiving constructive feedback and support from their peers.

Annual Album Release Party

The Annual Album Release Party is the highlight of the Club Cornell Music Production calendar. Each year, the club compiles and produces an album featuring original tracks created by its members. This album showcases the diverse talents and creative voices within the club, spanning various genres and styles. The release party is a celebration of this collective effort, featuring live performances of the tracks, DJ sets, and listening sessions. It's an exciting event that draws attention from the broader Cornell community and beyond, highlighting the club's commitment to nurturing musical talent and creativity. The Annual Album Release Party is not just about celebrating the finished product, but also about recognizing the hard work, dedication, and collaboration that went into its creation.

Connect with us on our socials to stay updated on upcoming events and opportunities, and become a part of a thriving community of music makers at Cornell University.
